T-shirt Printing

Dye-Sublimation Printing

Dye-Sublimation Printing is a computer printing technique which uses heat to transfer dye onto materials such as fabric.

Process

1.The picture is saved in svg file.

Image1: Picture in svg format

2. The picture is mirrored and printed.


Image2: Mirror Image of Picture

3.After that, the printed out picture is put onto the shirt and hot press at 170° Celcius for 45 seconds.

Image3: Hot Pressing For 45 Seconds

4. Lastly,the product is ready.
