Work Flow


Important Setting

  1. Toolpath Manager > Properties > Files
    Check for tool library and program number and set workpiece dimension.
  2. Menu Bar > Turning > Part Handling_Stock Adv>
    Blank original geometry
    Transferred Position: 1
    Stock Clearance: 1
  3. Rough Facing
    Stock to leave : 0.3
  4. OD Roughing
    Stock to leave in X : 0.8
    Stock to leave in Z : 0.1
    Stock Recognition: Use stock for outer boundary
    Lead out 4.5 (Extend)
  5. OD Grooving
    Use stock for outer boundarys
